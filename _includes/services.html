Code for Sri Lanka

Our vision is to improve technology that runs Sri Lanka by voluntarily helping the Government digitally transform by:

Creating Technology

Creating open source technology solutions that are necessary for the government to deploy key infrastructure at scale (e.g. email, collaboration, identity, and data & document archival)

Architecting Solutions

Helping government organizations architect digital platforms for themselves by looking at the long term goals of a digital Sri Lanka

Building Open Source Solutions

Working directly with government organizations to build vertical open source software solutions
